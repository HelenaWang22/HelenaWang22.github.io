I am a doctoral candidate at the Research Center of Children’s Reading and Learning within the Faculty of Psychology, Beijing Normal University. My supervisor is Professor Xinchun Wu. My academic creed is: be bold in strategy, be careful in tactics.

My current research interests are focused on:

  • Emotional and Cognitive Factors in Children's Reading Processing
  • Source Information Processing Mechanism in Multi-Text Reading Comprehension
  • The Influence of the Age of Intelligence on Teenagers' Language Ability Development
